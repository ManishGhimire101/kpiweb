About Kankai Polytechnic Institute

Leading Technical Education in Jhapa – Established 2019

Overview

Kankai Polytechnic Institute is a premier technical education institution located in Kankai, Jhapa, Nepal. Established in 2019, we are committed to providing quality diploma-level education in information technology, civil engineering, and geomatics engineering. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing skilled professionals who can contribute to national development and excel in the global economy.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education that empowers students with practical skills, professional knowledge, and ethical values for career success.

Our Vision

To be a leading polytechnic institution recognized for excellence in technical education and innovation in Nepal and South Asia.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Student-Centered Learning, and Community Engagement.
